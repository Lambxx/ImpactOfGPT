What we can do

Starting the conversation of the environmental impact of the technology, we take for granted is a step in the right direction. The majority of people do not think about the impact of the technology they use, a large majoirty of computation, from our messaging, our straming anw now ai aissitants; happens at a server out of sight from the use. This disconnect means as a society we have become dettatched from the real impact of our technology. With a rapildy worseining climate situation we need to seriously get the conversation going on ethical and sustainable technology. The data presented here is just a small snapshot. This site is an attempt to get that conversation going and i hope it inspires others to do the same.
